Install the App

Add to your home screen for quick access and offline use

Available On

iOS

Safari on iPhone & iPad

Android

Chrome on phones & tablets

How to Install

1

Open in Safari

Make sure you're using Safari browser on your iPhone or iPad

2

Tap the Share button

Look for the share icon (square with arrow) at the bottom of the screen

3

Select "Add to Home Screen"

Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen" option

4

Tap "Add" in the top right

The app will now appear on your home screen

1

Open in Chrome

Make sure you're using Chrome browser on your Android device

2

Tap the Menu button

Look for the three dots (⋮) in the top right corner

3

Select "Add to Home screen"

Or tap "Install app" if prompted at the bottom

4

Tap "Install"

The app will be added to your home screen
